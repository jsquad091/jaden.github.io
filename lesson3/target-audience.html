Target Audience:

People who want to know what the current weather is and what the future weather is going to be like. People who are wanting an easy, clean cut navigation experience when surfing the web. People looking for fast, simple data that is not confusing. Generally younger people that are more familiar with the internet will be the main target audience but the website will also be optimized for simplicity so that older people can browse the site as well.
